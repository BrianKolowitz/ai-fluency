# Competency Levels

Four levels of AI fluency, each building on the last. Designed for any organization — learners self-assess and enter at their level. No need to start at Beginner if you're already using AI regularly.

| Level | Focus | Entry Criteria |
|:------|:------|:---------------|
| [Beginner](./beginner.md) | Foundations & mindset | Zero AI experience |
| [Capable](./capable.md) | Applied use | Can write structured prompts |
| [Proficient](./proficient.md) | Workflow redesign | Uses AI weekly |
| [Expert](./expert.md) | Innovation & leadership | Has redesigned workflows with AI |

Every level includes hands-on exercises, a checkpoint, and a fluency slope reflection.

*Inspired by [Zapier's AI Fluency Rubric](https://zapier.com/blog/raising-ai-fluency-bar-in-hiring/).*
